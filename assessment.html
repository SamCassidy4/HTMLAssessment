  2. Quetzalcoatlus:
    3. More on Quetzalcoatlus
    • Quetzalcoatlus although not alive today, was one of the largest animals to exist. It measured as tall as 16ft and as long as 33 to 36ft.
    • Quetzalcoatlus is the largest flying creature to ever exist
    • Quetzalcoatlus lived 72.1 million years ago, and went extinct in the Cretacious period
    • Quetzalcoatlus was so large that it was unable to take off from the ground. It had to leap from at least 8ft to be able to take off
  3. Therizinosaurus:
    4. More on Therizinosaurus
    • Therizinosaurus or "Scythe lizard" lived in the late Cretaceous
    • The name "Scythe Lizard" comes from the three large claws this animal had on each of its hands
    • It used its claws for defense and and to reach vegetation
    • Therizinosaurus stood between 13 and 16 feet, and was as long as 33-40 feet.
